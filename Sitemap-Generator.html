Why Sitemaps Matter for SEO

Sitemaps help search engines discover and index your website content more efficiently. By providing a roadmap of your site, you ensure that all your important pages are crawled.

XML sitemaps are especially important for large websites, new websites, or sites with complex navigation structures.

SEO Best Practices

1. Update your sitemap regularly when you add new content

2. Submit your sitemap to Google Search Console

3. Include only canonical versions of your URLs

4. Keep your sitemap under 50,000 URLs

5. Compress large sitemaps using gzip
